
cyber-dojo is designed for group practice-sessions.
In this 'mode' URLs typically look like this...
   http://cyber-dojo.org/kata/edit/hVU93Kj8rq?avatar=tiger
The hVU93Kj8rq is a group-id
and the avatar=tiger is your animal inside this group
Underneath, the [group-id, avatar-name] map to a
kata with its own unique kata-id.

cyber-dojo can also be used for individual practice-sessions.
In this 'mode' URLs typically look like this...
  http://cyber-dojo.org/kata/edit/ym04AU98rH
The ym04AU98rH is a kata-id
and there is no avatar
